About Us

With over 20 years of professional cleaning experience, we know what it takes to get a space truly clean. Our background in restaurant operations and food safety taught us the highest standards, and we’ve carried that same level of care into everything we do.

After years of managing busy restaurant schedules, we made the decision to step away in favor of more time with our family. Moving back to our hometown of Penticton, BC, gave us more time with grandparents and made us realize how many seniors could use a helping hand. It’s a need we’re proud to meet.

As our business grew, so did the demand for our services. We quickly became the go-to team for large-scale cleaning jobs like residential move-outs and janitorial services for apartment buildings, condos, and strata properties. At the same time, we continue to offer routine housekeeping for seniors who need extra support, whether it’s weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

More than anything, we believe in great service. We’re friendly, easy to work with, and always focused on doing the job right. If you need a team that’s reliable, detail-oriented, and truly cares, we’d love to help.
